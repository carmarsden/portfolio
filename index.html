About Me

I am a web developer, though I wasn’t always. I love coding because it rewards clean logic, thoughtful design, and constant learning. In a past life I was my institution’s lead client for a number of SaaS products, while getting an MBA--so I’m afraid I can’t shake my client-first approach. Another thing that hasn’t changed: I like to start my day with a problem and end it with a solution.

If I’m "AFK" you can probably find me tabletop gaming, baking, or walking around Boston listening to personal finance podcasts. Otherwise, better find me on email.
